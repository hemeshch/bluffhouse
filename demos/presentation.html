the attention economy

Watching is spending.

Each street, every agent allocates a fixed attention budget across players and the table. What you watch, you catch. What you ignore goes dark.

Locked on the whisperer ~44%
Passive — just at the table ~23%
Watching the wrong player ~15%

p(intercept) ∝ base × ( 0.4 + focus_on_sender + 0.5·focus_on_target + 0.25·table_share )

the benchmark

Same harness.
Same tools.
Only the play differs.

Anonymized seats — models play "P1"…"P6", never "GPT". No name-recognition bias.
Duplicate format — every model plays the identical seeded deal, in every seat. Card luck cancels to zero.
No judges — every lie is recorded next to the sender's private intent. Manipulation that goes unnoticed just… wins.
